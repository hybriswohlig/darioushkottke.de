How It Works

Our wrap uses a proprietary blend of plant-derived and bio-based materials, enhanced with a natural antimicrobial additive made from upcycled agricultural waste. The result is a high-performance cling wrap that works just like conventional plastic — but returns to nature instead of polluting it.

Antibacterial Bonus: Our natural additive reduces food spoilage by up to 20%. Less spoilage means less food waste — and every kilogram of food waste avoided prevents roughly 3.4 kg of CO₂ emissions. This makes the wrap a carbon-multiplier in everyday use.

The Carbon Story

Conventional cling wrap is made from fossil fuels and incinerated at end of life, releasing all stored carbon back into the atmosphere. Our wrap takes a different path: it biodegrades naturally, returning carbon to the soil instead.

How We Calculated This

This report follows internationally recognised life cycle assessment (LCA) principles. Here is a brief overview of our approach:

Scope

Cradle-to-gate (production) plus end-of-life disposal. Covers all emissions from raw materials through final decomposition.

Standard

Aligned with ISO 14040/14044 and IPCC 2014 Global Warming Potential metrics. Third-party ISO 14040 audit planned for Q4 2026.

Data Sources

Peer-reviewed literature (Nature Sustainability, PlasticsEurope, Eurostat), TÜV Austria certifications, and EPA emission factors.

Comparison

Benchmarked against conventional LDPE cling wrap (2.58 kg roll, European production averages, 100% incineration at end-of-life).

Savings may vary based on actual disposal conditions, composting environment, and regional waste management infrastructure. Conservative assumptions used throughout. Full third-party verification via ISO 14040 audit planned Q4 2026.
