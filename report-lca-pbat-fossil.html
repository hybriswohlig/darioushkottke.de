1. Executive Summary

Bottom Line: As the EU advances towards stricter regulations under the Single-Use Plastics Directive and the Packaging & Packaging Waste Regulation (PPWR), N&E Innovations offers a sustainable alternative to conventional PVC and LDPE cling films. The Vikang-Enhanced Fossil-Based PBAT Cling Wrap combines a biodegradable polymer matrix with our proprietary upcycled antimicrobial additive. While the production footprint is comparable to conventional plastics, it eliminates the “Incineration Penalty” at end-of-life, promoting a Cradle-to-Cradle (Compostable) model over Cradle-to-Grave. Furthermore, its antibacterial properties act as a carbon-multiplier by reducing spoilage—the key to minimizing emissions in everyday use.

5. Methodology & Calculations

Transparency Statement: The LCA follows ISO 14040 principles, scoped to cradle-to-gate (production) plus end-of-life, using IPCC 2014 GWP metrics. Functional unit: one 2.58 kg roll. Biogenic CO₂ neutral; fossil-based PBAT emissions verified at 5.89 kg CO₂e/kg (cradle-to-gate).

Step-by-Step Calculations

Step 1: Upcycling Credit (Avoided Landfill Emissions)

Vikang per roll: 1% of 2.58 kg = 0.0258 kg Yield: 60%, so cashew shells diverted = 0.0258 / 0.6 = 0.043 kg Residue: 40% = 0.0172 kg. Total diverted = 0.043 + 0.0172 = 0.0602 kg Landfill avoidance factor: 0.1 kg CO₂e/kg (EPA WARM Model, 2023) Avoided emissions: 0.0602 × 0.1 = 0.006 kg CO₂e (rounded to -0.01)

Step 2: Transport Emissions

Distance: ~1,000 km sea freight (Vietnam to China) Factor: 0.016 kg CO₂e/tonne-km (Climatiq, 2023) Shells mass: 0.043 kg Emissions: (0.043 / 1000) × 1000 × 0.016 ≈ 0.0007 kg CO₂e (rounded to 0.01)

Step 3: Production (Cradle-to-Gate)

Conventional LDPE: 2.90 kg CO₂e/kg (Nature Sustainability, 2021) Per roll: 2.90 × 2.58 = 7.48 kg CO₂e Fossil PBAT: 5.89 kg CO₂e/kg (Sci. Total Environment, 2024, DOI: 10.1016/j.scitotenv.2024.176421) Gross: 5.89 × 2.58 = 15.1962 kg CO₂e Vikang adjustment: -0.086 kg (bio-additive efficiency) Net after upcycling: 15.1962 - 0.086 - 0.006 = 15.1042 kg CO₂e (≈ 15.10) Difference: 7.48 - 15.1042 = +7.62 kg CO₂e (investment)

Step 4: End-of-Life

Theoretical CO₂ for LDPE: 3.1 kg CO₂/kg (86% carbon; PlasticsEurope, 2023) Conventional (100% incinerated): 3.1 × 2.58 = 8.00 kg CO₂e Theoretical CO₂ for PBAT: 2.1 kg CO₂/kg (57% carbon content) Vikang-FossilPBAT (90% biodegraded): Emissions from 10% non-biodegraded = 2.1 × 0.1 × 2.58 = 0.5418 kg CO₂e (≈ 0.54) Net savings: 8.00 - 0.54 = 7.46 kg CO₂e

Step 5: Total Lifecycle

Conventional LDPE: 7.48 (production) + 8.00 (EOL) = 15.48 kg CO₂e Vikang-FossilPBAT: 15.1042 (production) + 0.54 (EOL) + 0.001 = 15.6449 kg CO₂e Difference: 15.48 - 15.6449 = +0.16 kg CO₂e (neutral)
