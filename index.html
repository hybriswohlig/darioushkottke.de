Key Definitions

CO2e (Carbon Dioxide Equivalent)
A measure that converts all greenhouse gases into an equivalent amount of CO2 based on their warming potential.
Cradle-to-Gate
Life cycle stage from raw material extraction to product leaving the factory gate (excludes use and disposal).
Upcycling
Converting waste (e.g., food waste) into a valuable product (e.g., Vikang additive), avoiding emissions from waste disposal.
Biodegradation Rate
The percentage of material broken down by microbes in compost, measured by CO2 release from ASTM D6400 test.
Theoretical CO2 (ThCO2)
Maximum CO2 that could be released if 100% of the material's carbon is oxidized. For PVC: ~1.41 kg CO2 per kg.
Landfill Emissions Factor
Emissions from waste decomposing in landfills (mostly methane). For general food waste: ~4.42 kg CO2e/kg (UN FAO).
