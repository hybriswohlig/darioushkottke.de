Liquid and Powder Disinfectant
Prepared by: Greenly for N&E Innovations
Date: October 15, 2025 | Version: 1.0
Focus: Cradle-to-Gate Climate Impact Analysis

Executive Summary

Bottom Line: Our cashew-testa based disinfectants show significant environmental benefits. The liquid solution (1L) emits 766 gCO2e, while the powder (1kg) emits 1.7 kgCO2e. Compared to ethanol-based alternatives, raw material emissions are reduced by ~64%. Upcycling cashew-testa avoids up to 140 gCO2e per 0.2kg vs. landfill. Extending spray bottle reuse to 3 years cuts emissions to 486 gCO2e per 1L. This report highlights opportunities for sustainable procurement and material optimization to further reduce impacts.

Key Insights

The analysis uses a cradle-to-gate approach, focusing on raw materials and manufacturing. Packaging contributes significantly (56.7% for liquid), while upcycling cashew-testa provides clear avoidance benefits over traditional waste treatments.

Strategic Value

  • Sustainability: Lower emissions than ethanol-based disinfectants
  • Upcycling: Turns waste into value, avoiding landfill emissions
  • Reuse Potential: Extended bottle life reduces per-use impact by ~37%

Limitations

Excludes transport, use, and end-of-life stages. Recommendations include supplier engagement and packaging optimization for further reductions.

Sensitivity: Impacts vary with disposal methods; upcycling minimizes emissions compared to alternatives.

Methodology

LCA Framework

Approach: Cradle-to-gate (extraction of raw materials and manufacturing). Excludes distribution, use, transport, and end-of-life stages.

Action Plan

Implement Carbon Impact Conditions in Purchase Policy

Industry: All | Parent Category: ProductPurchases

  • Establish and start monitoring KPIs (e.g., % suppliers with carbon footprints, roadmaps aligned to Paris Agreement)
  • Identify eco-conditions for purchase policy (SMART goals)
  • Support suppliers with tools, training, and resources

Impact: Medium, long-term; reduces Scope 3 emissions.

Benchmarks: 1.5¬∞C Supply Chain Leaders; Livent supplier assessments.

Optimize Use of Materials & Reduce Offcuts

Industry: All | Parent Category: ProductPurchases

  • Analyze processes to identify improvements
  • Develop and implement solutions with action plans and KPIs
  • Monitor and continuously improve

Impact: Medium, medium-term; 10-30% CO2e reduction (up to 90% in benchmarks).

Benchmarks: Interface's "Mission Zero".

Reduce the Weight of Your Packaging

Industry: All | Parent Category: ProductPurchases

  • Assess current packaging for inefficiencies
  • Streamline to minimize weight while ensuring protection
  • Involve stakeholders and communicate sustainability goals

Impact: Medium, medium-term; 5-20% reduction in packaging impact.

Benchmarks: Seventh Generation; Lush "naked" packaging.

Choose Packaging Made from Recycled Raw Materials

Industry: All | Parent Category: ProductPurchases

  • Evaluate current materials and impacts
  • Study replacements under operational constraints
  • Find sustainable suppliers

Impact: Up to 90% reduction in packaging emissions; short-term.

Benchmarks: Patagonia; IKEA (100% recycled/renewable by 2030).
